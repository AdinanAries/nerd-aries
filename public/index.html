Get a highly customized website for your business

You put efforts into ensuring quality of products and services for your business – wouldn’t you do the same for your website?

Using a free template for your website may sound great but have you considered the trade-off that is. I've listed below few of the good reasons why you should always get a custom website.

Firstly, a custom website is unique to only your business and you can actually protect the design with Copyright. It has features specific to only your business and identifies your unique business with a unique style.

Also, When you hire a programmer to build a custom website for your business, he takes the time to get to know you and your business. A free template can not begin to reflect what you want to portray about your business. The programmer will take the time to get to know what you want and create a site that reflects that vision.

A custom site is usually optimized for higher ranking in search engines. Custom sites are specially designed to appear higher in search engines, giving you an edge over your competitors. The developer can help you become more visible on the internet by creating a tailor made website that attracts the right people.

Further more, since you're hiring the developer to build your site, you can also count on them to maintain it for your. Even better, he can show you how to do it yourself.
Also, Configuring and updating a free website is time consuming. Templates work great when you want to copy exactly what’s there. But put in your own information, and one size doesn’t always fit all.

With a custom website, it is feasible to coordinate brand recognition through social media, print media and web design. This indicates that everything that has to do with your business can look the same and boost your visual recognition – which in turn will boost your business.

Lastly, a custom website is adaptable to your business as it grows. This means that you can start small and perhaps later add a blog, e-commerce, photo gallery, or application? A custom website can grow with you.

Your business is one of a kind. Don’t blend in with other businesses using the same template. Your business is unique – let’s show everyone how it stands apart from the rest.

About

Hey, I'm Mohammed Adinan, aka Nerd Aries. Somewhere In 2011, I started out in IT as a computer hardware and networking engineer, and also database administrator. Three years later, perhaps of doing mostly database stuff and writing SQL code, I figured my passion is programming, so I decided to go full-time in the direction of software.

Over the last five years I've been actively writing code and building applications and websites. I've also been playing my part in helping to enrich the industry with new talents by encouraging and helping the begginers. I've also taken part in the training of many students from various bootcamps and colleges who needed help as they were struggling with their course materials.

Having both the theoretical and practical knowledge on designing and creating software, I'm able to desing, build, deploy and maintain web sites and applications. This means that, as a self-made software engineer and architect, I design system software architecture, build systems fullstack-wise with modern frontend tech, and usually a java and/or node.js backend, powered by Microsoft SQL server or MySQL database.
