Get your customized business websites

Think of your website as a fancy car.

When rating a car, we often think about its features which make it better or worse than other cars. Some of the features are about the looks, the engine capacity, its efficiency, how comfortable it is inside, etc... you get the idea... This means that the external beauty of the car is only one of many things that can make it a great car, and of course, can determine how much the car costs.

When it comes to building your website, the statement above implies that the measure of your website's good and cost is going to be based not only on the design or looks, but also, on what lies "under the hood" that makes it work.

It is also worth noting that, with your website's appearnace, having a truly unique design will really set you apart from all other business websites. And also, depending on the features you want on your website, the amount of work needed to build the backend(engine) of your website can be estimated by the number and complexity of those features.

Hiring experienced developer(s) to build your website from scratch can be expensive at times, with the two main cost-influencing factors being the type of design and the kinds of features, where features can be defined as "the functions that your website provides for your employees, customers, and other website visitors". It is also important to note that, as the amount and complexity of features on your website grows, the design part may gradually loose much significance on the overall cost of building and maintaining the website.

At NerdAries, we always work around our clients budget, making sure that they get the highest possible value from their expenditure. This implies that, we do a thorough exploration of the ecosystem, combining, comparing and contrasting different technologies to produce a package that fits their exact budget.
