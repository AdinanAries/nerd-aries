Get your customized business websites

As a business owner you are always looking for ways to save money and make your business more profitable. So using a free generic website template may seem like a great option to save on your bottom line. You put care into the quality of the products and services you provide – wouldn’t you do the same for your website? Here are some reasons why you need a custom website – and shouldn’t settle for just a template.

1. We take the time to get to know you and your business. A free template can not begin to reflect what you want to portray about your business. We will take the time to get to know what YOU want and create a site that reflects that vision.
2. Higher Ranking in Search Engines. Custom sites are specially designed to appear higher in search engines, giving you an edge over your competitors. We can help you become more visible on the internet by creating a tailor made website that attracts the right people.
3. We are here for you after your custom site launches. We are happy to help you maintain your site, or show you how to do it yourself. In fact, we included training with each site we design and are here to answer any questions you may have.
4. Configuring and updating a free website is time consuming. Templates work great when you want to copy exactly what’s there. But put in your own information, and one size doesn’t always fit all.
5. Coordinated brand recognition through social media, print media and web design. That’s right! Everything that has to do with your business can look the same and boost your visual recognition – which in turn will boost your business.
6. Adaptable to your business as it grows. Want to start small and perhaps later add a blog, e-commerce, photo gallery, or application? A custom website can grow with you.
7. Access to premium features and design. As professional designers and web developers, we have access to professional versions of most frameworks and plugins that we share with our clients at no extra cost.
8. Your business is one of a kind. Don’t blend in with other businesses using the same template. Your business is unique – let’s show everyone how it stands apart from the rest.
9. Stronger Business Credibility. What are competitors and potential customers going to think when they see the name of a cookie cutter “free” template in your footer of your homepage? It most certainly will not stand out among the millions of other businesses who used the same template or speak to the uniqueness of your business.
10. Something To Be Proud Of. You’re proud of your business. Let’s show it off to the rest of the world.
