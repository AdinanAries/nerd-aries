I'm your learning mentor


Whether you're studying CS in college or enrolled in a bootcamp, or self-teaching yourself programming, one thing holds true, in any case, you need to put in hours and hours of work to develop your skills.

Software is one of the highly paying industries where a college degree isn't required to land your first job.

However, in order to get a programming job, you need to be equipped with specific skills required in the industry.

This skills-set comes in the form of trending technologies you need to know, specific CS topics you need to master, and also your ability to understand the tech ecosystem properly.

I offer free mentorship for you so that you can accelerate your learning and preparation towards your first programming job.
